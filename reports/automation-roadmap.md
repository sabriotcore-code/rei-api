# REI Collections Automation Roadmap

## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive automation roadmap for REI collections operations across three interconnected systems: Collections Management System (CMS), Customer Relationship Management (CRM), and Financial Reporting System (FRS). The roadmap prioritizes high-impact, measurable improvements to workflow efficiency, customer experience, data accuracy, and operational cost reduction.

## Current State Assessment

### System Overview
- **Collections Management System (CMS)**: Core collections workflow management
- **Customer Relationship Management (CRM)**: Customer interaction and communication tracking
- **Financial Reporting System (FRS)**: Financial metrics and reporting

### Key Challenges Identified
1. Manual data synchronization between systems
2. Inconsistent customer communication workflows
3. Limited real-time visibility into collection performance
4. Resource-intensive manual reporting processes
5. Lack of automated escalation procedures
