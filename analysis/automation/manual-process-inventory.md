# Manual Process Inventory

## Objective
Identify and document manual processes within the REI collections operations that can be considered for automation.

## Manual Processes

### Customer Payment Reminder
- **System:** System A
- **Current Process:** Manual sending of emails and SMS to remind customers of their due payments.
- **Frequency:** Daily
- **Pain Points:** Time-consuming and prone to human errors.

### Data Validation for New Accounts
- **System:** System B
- **Current Process:** Manual checking of new account details for accuracy and completeness.
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Pain Points:** High chances of oversight and inconsistencies.

### Invoice Processing
- **System:** System C
- **Current Process:** Manual processing of invoices received in emails and physical copies.
- **Frequency:** Continuous
- **Pain Points:** High manual effort required for data entry, error-prone.

### Customer Feedback Collection
- **System:** System A
- **Current Process:** Manually collecting customer feedback through surveys and email follow-ups.
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Pain Points:** Low response rate and inconsistent data.
