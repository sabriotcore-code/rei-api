# Scoring Criteria for Business Impact Assessment

The following scoring criteria will be used to assess the business impact of identified gaps and automation opportunities across the REI collections operations:

## Scoring System

Each metric is scored on a scale of 1 to 5, where:

- **1:** Negligible impact
- **2:** Minimal impact
- **3:** Moderate impact
- **4:** Significant impact
- **5:** Transformational impact

## Scoring Categories

### 1. Workflow Efficiency
- **Tasks Time Reduction:**
  - 1: Less than 1 hour/week
  - 2: 1-2 hours/week
  - 3: 3-5 hours/week
  - 4: 6-10 hours/week
  - 5: More than 10 hours/week

- **Throughput Increase:**
  - 1: Less than 2 cases/week
  - 2: 2-5 cases/week
  - 3: 6-10 cases/week
  - 4: 11-20 cases/week
  - 5: More than 20 cases/week

### 2. Customer Experience
- **Satisfaction Score Change:**
  - 1: Less than 0.5% improvement
  - 2: 0.5%-1% improvement
  - 3: 1%-2% improvement
  - 4: 2%-3% improvement
  - 5: More than 3% improvement

- **Complaint Reduction:**
  - 1: Less than 1 complaint reduced/month
  - 2: 1-2 complaints/month
  - 3: 3-5 complaints/month
  - 4: 6-10 complaints/month
  - 5: More than 10 complaints/month

### 3. Data Accuracy
- **Error Rate Reduction:**
  - 1: Less than 0.1% reduction
  - 2: 0.1%-0.2% reduction
  - 3: 0.2%-0.5% reduction
  - 4: 0.5%-1% reduction
  - 5: More than 1% reduction

- **Consistent Data Updates:**
  - 1: Less than 80% conformity
  - 2: 80%-85% conformity
  - 3: 85%-90% conformity
  - 4: 90%-95% conformity
  - 5: 95%-100% conformity

### 4. Cost Reduction
- **Labor Cost Savings:**
  - 1: Less than $500/month
  - 2: $500-$1,000/month
  - 3: $1,000-$2,000/month
  - 4: $2,000-$5,000/month
  - 5: More than $5,000/month

- **Technology Costs Reduction:**
  - 1: Less than $100/month
  - 2: $100-$200/month
  - 3: $200-$500/month
  - 4: $500-$1,000/month
  - 5: More than $1,000/month
