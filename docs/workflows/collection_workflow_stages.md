# Collection Workflow Stages

## Overview
The collection workflow stages are the various steps that a collection process goes through from initial assignment to completion. The following sections outline the current workflow stages, status transitions, and decision criteria used in the PME Master sheet.

## Workflow Stages
1. **Assignment**: The collection process is assigned to a team or individual.
2. **Planning**: The team plans the collection process, including identifying the data to be collected and the methods to be used.
3. **Data Collection**: The team collects the data according to the plan.
4. **Data Review**: The team reviews the collected data for quality and completeness.
5. **Data Analysis**: The team analyzes the collected data to identify trends and patterns.
6. **Reporting**: The team creates a report based on the analysis.
7. **Review and Approval**: The report is reviewed and approved by stakeholders.
8. **Completion**: The collection process is complete and the report is finalized.

## Status Transitions
The following status transitions are used in the PME Master sheet:
| Current Status | Next Status | Decision Criteria |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Assigned | Planned | Assignment is accepted by the team |
| Planned | In Progress | Planning is complete and data collection can begin |
| In Progress | In Review | Data collection is complete |
| In Review | Analyzing | Data review is complete and data is of sufficient quality |
| Analyzing | Reported | Data analysis is complete |
| Reported | Approved | Report is reviewed and approved by stakeholders |
| Approved | Complete | Report is finalized |

## Status Codes
The following status codes are used in the PME Master sheet:
* Assigned (AS)
* Planned (PL)
* In Progress (IP)
* In Review (IR)
* Analyzing (AN)
* Reported (RP)
* Approved (AP)
* Complete (CM)
