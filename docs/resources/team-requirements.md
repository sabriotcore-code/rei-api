# Team Requirements
## Introduction
The rei-api automation project requires a skilled team with a mix of technical and business acumen. The team composition will play a crucial role in the successful implementation of the top 3 automation priorities.
## Team Composition
The team will consist of:
* 1 Project Manager
* 2 Software Engineers (Automation)
* 1 Quality Assurance Engineer
* 1 Technical Writer
* 1 Business Analyst
## Skill Sets
The team members should possess the following skill sets:
* Programming languages: Python, Java, JavaScript
* Automation frameworks: Selenium, Appium, Pytest
* Agile methodologies: Scrum, Kanban
* Cloud platforms: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud
* Strong understanding of rei-api and its integrations
## Responsibilities
* Project Manager: Overall project planning, coordination, and stakeholder management
* Software Engineers (Automation): Design, develop, and test automation scripts
* Quality Assurance Engineer: Develop and execute test cases, ensure quality and reliability
* Technical Writer: Create and maintain technical documentation
* Business Analyst: Define business requirements, ensure alignment with business objectives
